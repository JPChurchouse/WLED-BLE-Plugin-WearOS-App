## Full description

WLED BLE Wear lets you control your WLED-powered LED setup directly from your
Wear OS smartwatch using Bluetooth Low Energy — no Wi-Fi, no internet, no fuss.
Designed to work alongside the WLED BLE plugin, this app gives you quick,
low-latency control of your lights without ever needing to reach for your phone.

────────────────────────────────────────
FEATURES
────────────────────────────────────────

● Connect directly to your WLED BLE device from your wrist
● Toggle power on and off with a clear, easy-to-read switch
● Browse and activate all of your saved WLED presets from a scrollable list
● Low-latency Bluetooth control — no network, no cloud, no delay
● Automatic reconnection — if your watch loses contact with the device, it
  reconnects silently in the background so you're always ready
● Clean, minimal Wear OS interface built with Jetpack Compose
● Works entirely offline

────────────────────────────────────────
WEAR OS INTEGRATION
────────────────────────────────────────

WledBleWear is built for your wrist first. Beyond the main app, it extends into
your watch face and tile carousel for quick access wherever you are.

TILE
Add the WLED tile to your carousel for instant, glanceable control. The tile
shows your device name, current power state, and active preset — and updates
automatically as your connection changes. Tap to jump straight to the control
screen and reconnect if needed. If you were connected when you last used the
app, the tile will show your last known state so you always have context at a
glance.

COMPLICATIONS — four options to suit your watch face:

  ⬡ Icon — a compact lightbulb icon for small slots. Tap to connect and open
    the control screen.

  ⬡ Device Name — your device name alongside the icon in a short-text slot.
    Tap to connect and go.

  ⬡ Power Toggle — shows On or Off with a matching icon reflecting your
    current power state. Tap to toggle power silently in the background — no
    app launch, no interruption. If your device isn't already connected, the
    watch reconnects automatically before applying the change.

  ⬡ Status — a read-only complication showing your device name and active
    preset name (or last known state when out of range). Perfect for a
    persistent at-a-glance view of your lighting setup.

────────────────────────────────────────
REQUIREMENTS
────────────────────────────────────────

● A Wear OS smartwatch running Wear OS 3.0 or later
● A WLED device running the companion WLED BLE plugin:
  https://github.com/JPChurchouse/WLED-BLE-Plugin.git

────────────────────────────────────────
PRIVACY
────────────────────────────────────────

This app collects no data, requires no account, and makes no network requests.
All communication is directly between your watch and your device over Bluetooth.
Nothing leaves your home.
