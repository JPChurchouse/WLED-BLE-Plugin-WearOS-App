# Privacy Policy

**App:** WLED BLE Wear
**Developer:** Jamie Churchouse (JPChurchouse)
**Last updated:** April 2026

---

## Overview

WLED BLE Wear is a Wear OS application that communicates with a custom Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) plugin for [WLED](https://kno.wled.ge/) to allow control of WLED-powered devices directly from a smartwatch.

---

## Data Collection

**This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Specifically:

- No account registration or login is required
- No personal information is collected
- No usage analytics or telemetry are gathered
- No crash reporting data is sent to any server
- No data is stored on-device beyond what the Android/Wear OS operating system manages automatically (e.g. the app installation itself)

---

## Bluetooth

The app uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to communicate with a local BLE peripheral device running the WLED BLE plugin. This communication is:

- **Local only** — between your watch and your BLE device on the same physical proximity
- **Not transmitted over the internet**
- **Not logged or recorded** by this app

The BLE permission is used solely to discover and communicate with the WLED BLE peripheral.

---

## Network Access

This app does **not** make any network requests. No data is sent to any remote server, cloud service, or third party.

---

## Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external services.

---

## Children's Privacy

This app does not collect data from any users, including children. It is compliant with COPPA and equivalent regulations by virtue of collecting no data whatsoever.
